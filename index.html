Justice for Us

Hey there, my name is Aileen. I work at the ACLU as a junior web developer. I recently met Davina at the Allied Media Conference, an event that brings together activists from all over the United States to share tools and tactics for transforming our communities through media based organizing. After meeting Davina, I wanted to connect with her to work on similar projects we are working on and tap into her list of contacts. The challenge of our schedules, locations, and resources led us to create Justice for Us - a microblog.
