Justice For Us

Hey there, my name is Aileen. I work at the ACLU as a junior web developer. I recently met Davina at the Allied Media Conference, an event that brings together activists from all over the United States to share tools and tactics for transforming our communities through media based organizing. After meeting Davina, I wanted to connect with her to work on similar projects and tap into her list of contacts. The distance, schedules, and resources made it hard to connect in person or by phone. So we had the idea of building a social networking site for activists like us.
